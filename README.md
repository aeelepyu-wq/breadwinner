# Breadwinner
Here's a game that I made quite a while ago. There was a big time gap between me posting this and me actually making the game, coding slump, I guess? 

You live in a world so simple that everything is filled with simplicity. You're a fresh graduate of your high school, there isn't any university or grad school in this world, and you so happen to be a maths problem solver, still an entry-level job.  Your job is to, of course, solve math problems, specifically addition, as you are still at an entry-level position. 

While doing your corporate job (yes, a maths solver is a corporate job in this economy), you've always dreamt of eating bread every day. Why? No one knows, it's just your signature cup of tea to who you are.

Enjoy the game! ^^
